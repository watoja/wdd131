Masaka, Uganda

"Oh Uganda, may God uphold thee" - a phrase that echoes the country's rich history, diverse culture, and breathtaking natural beauty. Located in East Africa, Uganda is a landlocked nation bordered by Kenya, South Sudan, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Rwanda, and Tanzania, with Kampala as its vibrant capital. From the snow-capped Rwenzori Mountains to the lush forests of Bwindi Impenetrable National Park, home to endangered mountain gorillas, Uganda's varied terrain is a treasure trove of wildlife and natural wonders. With a strong agricultural sector, significant natural resources, and a growing tourism industry, its rich cultural heritage, reflected in its over 50 languages and vibrant traditional music and dance, Uganda continues to inspire and captivate visitors from around the world in droves.
